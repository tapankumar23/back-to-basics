Email Service & Leaderboard

Distributed email delivery pipelines and real-time leaderboard design

11.1 — Distributed Email Service Campaign ingestion, delivery workers, and retry pipelines
Email is a throughput-heavy, reputation-sensitive system. The pipeline needs ingestion APIs, durable queues, template rendering, rate-aware SMTP dispatch, feedback loops for bounces and complaints, and retry controls so one bad provider event does not stall the whole campaign.
11.2 — Real-time Gaming Leaderboard Hot-path ranking with sorted sets and durable event logs
Leaderboards need ultra-fast score updates and reads, so the ranking lives in memory on the hot path, often using a sorted set. Durable logs and periodic snapshots protect against loss, while fanout layers cache top-N views for game clients around the world.
