Back-of-Envelope Interview Framework

Interview Foundations

Back-of-the-envelope estimation and a structured system design problem-solving framework

10.1 — Back-of-the-Envelope Estimation Translate product assumptions into capacity numbers fast
Estimation is about showing order-of-magnitude thinking. In an interview you rarely need exactness; you need believable assumptions, clear arithmetic, and the ability to turn DAU, requests per user, payload size, and retention into QPS, storage, bandwidth, and capacity targets.
10.2 — System Design Problem-Solving Framework Drive the conversation with structure instead of improvisation
This framework is a sequencing tool: clarify the problem, estimate scale, sketch the high-level design, then go deep where risk lives. This keeps the interview collaborative and prevents premature optimization or wandering into low-value detail too early.
